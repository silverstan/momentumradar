About the France market

Euronext Paris hosts the CAC 40, an index dominated by global champions in luxury goods (LVMH, Hermès, Kering), aerospace (Airbus, Safran), energy (TotalEnergies) and cosmetics (L'Oréal). Because its constituents sell worldwide, the CAC 40 often behaves more like a global consumer index than a purely French one.
