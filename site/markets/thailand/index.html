About the Thailand market

The Stock Exchange of Thailand blends energy heavyweights (PTT group), leading regional banks, hospital operators serving Asia's medical-tourism boom, and export electronics stars like Delta Electronics. Tourism recovery cycles and energy prices are the two big momentum drivers in Bangkok.
