About the Australia market

The ASX 200 blends the world's biggest miners (BHP, Fortescue, Rio Tinto) with a highly concentrated banking sector and standout global healthcare and technology firms like CSL, Pro Medicus and WiseTech. Australia's market is a favourite of income investors thanks to franking credits and high dividend payouts.
