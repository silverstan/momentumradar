About the Hong Kong market

HKEX is the primary venue where global investors access China's technology, financial and consumer giants — Tencent, Alibaba, Meituan, BYD and the big state banks all trade here. The Hang Seng Index is highly sensitive to Chinese policy shifts and global rate expectations, which makes momentum signals here especially dynamic.
