About the Indonesia market

The Indonesia Stock Exchange rides the growth of Southeast Asia's largest economy and its 270-million population. Big banks (BCA, BRI, Mandiri) sit alongside world-scale nickel, coal and copper producers — a mix that gives Jakarta both structural consumer growth and powerful commodity-driven momentum cycles.
