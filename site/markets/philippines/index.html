About the Philippines market

The Philippine Stock Exchange is characterised by powerful family conglomerates — SM, Ayala, JG Summit, Aboitiz — spanning banking, property, retail and utilities. With one of Asia's youngest populations and strong remittance flows, consumer-facing names dominate the momentum leaderboard in Manila.
