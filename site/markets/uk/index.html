About the United Kingdom market

The London Stock Exchange is one of the world's oldest exchanges and remains Europe's most international market, with FTSE 100 constituents earning around three-quarters of their revenue overseas. The index is rich in energy, mining, banking and consumer-staples giants, giving it a very different character from the tech-heavy US market.
