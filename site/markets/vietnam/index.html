About the Vietnam market

The Ho Chi Minh Stock Exchange sits at the centre of one of the world's most exciting growth stories: Vietnam's manufacturing boom, rapid urbanisation and an expected upgrade from frontier to emerging-market status. Banks, steel, technology outsourcing (FPT) and real estate dominate the leaderboard, with foreign-ownership limits adding a unique supply-demand dynamic.
