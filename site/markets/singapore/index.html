About the Singapore market

SGX anchors Southeast Asia's capital markets. The Straits Times Index is dominated by the three local banks (DBS, OCBC, UOB) — together roughly half the index — plus a deep bench of real-estate investment trusts that make Singapore Asia's REIT hub. A stable currency and strong governance make it a defensive favourite in the region.
