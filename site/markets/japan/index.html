About the Japan market

The Tokyo Stock Exchange is Asia's largest developed market. Japan's corporate governance reforms — pushing companies to lift returns on equity, unwind cross-shareholdings and return cash to shareholders — have reignited global investor interest. The market mixes world-leading automation and semiconductor-equipment makers with iconic consumer brands.
