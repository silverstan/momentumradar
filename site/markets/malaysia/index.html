About the Malaysia market

Bursa Malaysia lists over 900 companies, with the KLCI dominated by banks (Maybank, CIMB, Public Bank), utilities (Tenaga Nasional), plantations and oil & gas services. Malaysia's data-centre construction boom and stable dividend payers have drawn renewed foreign interest to Kuala Lumpur in recent years.
