About the Germany market

Germany is Europe's largest economy and the DAX 40 reflects its industrial DNA: automotive, engineering, chemicals, insurance and increasingly software and defence. Traded on Deutsche Börse's XETRA platform in Frankfurt, the DAX is a total-return index — a detail many beginners miss when comparing it with price-only indices like the FTSE 100.
