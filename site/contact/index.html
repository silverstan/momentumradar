✉️ Contact MomentumRadar

Feedback, corrections, partnership enquiries — we'd love to hear from you. The best way to reach us is by email:

hello@momentumradar.net

We aim to respond to all enquiries within 2–3 business days. We operate across multiple time zones (base: UTC+8), so replies outside business hours may take a little longer.

Types of enquiries we handle

Data corrections

If you believe any figure on MomentumRadar is incorrect, email us the stock ticker, the figure you believe is wrong, and the correct figure with your source. We take data accuracy seriously and aim to investigate factual errors within 24 hours.

Editorial feedback

Suggestions for the Education Hub, glossary or FAQ are always welcome — we're continually improving the beginner content.

Market coverage requests

We currently cover 13 markets across the US, Europe, Asia-Pacific and ASEAN. Want to see India (NSE/BSE), South Korea (KOSPI), Brazil (B3) or another market added? Let us know — expansion is guided by reader demand.

Advertising & partnerships

For advertising, sponsorship or business partnerships, email hello@momentumradar.net with the subject line "Partnership Enquiry". We respond within 3–5 business days.

Press & media

Journalists and creators seeking background on momentum investing or the markets we cover are welcome to reach out for data, commentary and context.

What we cannot help with

As a data and education platform we cannot provide personalised investment advice, recommend what you should buy or sell, or advise on your financial situation — for that, consult a qualified, licensed financial adviser in your jurisdiction. We also can't provide real-time prices: all data reflects the most recent daily site update.
