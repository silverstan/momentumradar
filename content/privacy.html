🔒 Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how MomentumRadar ("we", "us", accessible at momentumradar.net) handles information when you visit the site.

Information we collect

MomentumRadar requires no account and collects no personal information directly. You can use every feature without providing your name, email address, or any identifying details. If you email us at hello@momentumradar.net, we receive the information you choose to send and use it only to respond to your enquiry.

Cookies and advertising

We display advertising served by Google AdSense to keep the site free. Google and its partners may use cookies and similar technologies to serve ads based on your visits to this and other websites. Specifically:

  • Third-party vendors, including Google, use cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to this website or other websites.
  • Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads to you based on your visits to this site and/or other sites on the Internet.
  • You may opt out of personalised advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings, or opt out of some third-party vendors' cookies at www.aboutads.info.

Where required by law (including the EEA and UK), a consent message will be shown before any advertising cookies are set, and you may refuse or withdraw consent at any time.

Analytics and server logs

Our hosting provider may automatically log standard technical information (IP address, browser type, pages requested, timestamps) for security and operational purposes. We do not use this information to identify individuals.

Third-party links

Stock cards link to external sites such as Yahoo Finance, and flags/fonts load from third-party CDNs (flagcdn.com, Google Fonts). Those services have their own privacy policies; we are not responsible for their practices.

Children's privacy

This site provides financial education for a general audience and is not directed at children under 16. We knowingly collect no data from children.

Your rights

Depending on your jurisdiction (e.g. GDPR in the EU/UK, CCPA in California, PDPA in Malaysia/Singapore), you may have rights to access, correct or delete personal data. Since we hold essentially none beyond email correspondence you initiate, requests can be sent to hello@momentumradar.net and will be handled within 30 days.

Changes

We may update this policy from time to time; the "last updated" date above will change accordingly. Continued use of the site constitutes acceptance of the current policy.
